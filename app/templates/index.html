What's a compliment sandwich?

It's a way to give a criticism to a narcissist, because they refuse or are unable to hear anything but compliments.

How does it work?

1. Give them a compliment and they will begin paying attention

2. With their attention focused, deploy the critique. Warning: attention levels will immediately drop

3. Before focus is lost, give them another compliment. There is an increased possibility they will have registered the critique.

Why Donald Trump?

This is a popular technique in the workplace, so why not apply it to the halls of power? A compliment that will hook Trump can be very simple: mindless jingoism, pure fiction that benefits him, or just his name rendered in shiny dollar signs.

What's a compliment sandwich?

It's a way to give a criticism to a narcissist, because they refuse or are unable to hear anything but compliments.

How does it work?

1. Give them a compliment and they will begin paying attention

2. With their focus now on your words, deliver a critique. Warning: attention levels will immediately drop

3. Before you lose them, say another compliment. There is a better chance they will have heard the critique in the middle.

Why Donald Trump?

This is a popular technique in the workplace, so why not apply it to the halls of power? A compliment that will hook Trump can be extremely simple: mindless jingoism, pure lies, or just his name rendered in shiny dollar signs.
